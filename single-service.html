What Makes Us Different?

CCARROLL Construction Management translates visions into reality, managing value-add and development projects from ideation to design to construction.

Our services begin with a thorough due diligence process, collecting market knowledge that enables specialized ideation and design of a project’s value-creation opportunities. Scalable scope creation paired with detailed renderings bring projects to life as our team leverages national relationships to competitively bid services to reduce pricing and timing, and increase quality.

Full-Scope Construction

Our in-house construction and development team has vast experience overseeing the construction of real estate projects nationwide – handling internal claims work, insurance reconciliation, recommendations, payments, scope design, advisement on BI calculations, and submittals – on ground-up construction of apartment homes, student housing and mixed use developments, and value-add repositioning of existing properties.

CUSTOMER journey

Full-Cycle

Our in-house design and architectural team has significant experience in identifying value-creation opportunities and translating vision into effective, revenue-enhancing design concepts.

National Scale

With developments completed in Georgia, North Carolina, South Carolina, Florida, Tennessee, Texas, Kansas, Alabama, and Louisiana, CARROLL Construction prides itself on its national scale. We utilize collaborations with architects, engineers, local government officials, and our in-house construction management personnel to manage projects in any market across the United States.
